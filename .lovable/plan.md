
# Don Cheffy - Landing Page

A single-page marketing website inspired by the Luffu.com layout, adapted for Don Cheffy — an AI-powered recipe and shopping list app.

## Structure & Sections

### 1. Hero Section
- Full-screen with warm food-themed gradient background (creams, greens matching app palette)
- Large headline: **"Cocina sin complicaciones. Don Cheffy lo resuelve."**
- Subtitle explaining the value: AI generates recipes and builds your shopping list automatically
- CTA button: "Únete a la lista de espera" (or App Store link)
- Floating question bubbles (like Luffu's heads) with food emojis and questions like: "¿Qué cocino hoy?", "¿Qué me falta en el súper?", "¿Qué desayuno con lo que tengo?"

### 2. Problem Statement Section
- Title: "Para los que odian pensar qué cocinar"
- Copy about the daily struggle of meal planning and grocery shopping

### 3. Feature Showcase - AI Recipe Generation
- Phone mockup showing the AI generator screen (from uploaded screenshot)
- Title: "Recetas generadas con IA"
- Description of how it works: describe what you want, AI creates the recipe

### 4. Feature Showcase - Smart Shopping List
- Phone mockup showing the shopping list screen
- Title: "Tu lista de compras, automática"
- Highlights: ingredients aggregated from multiple recipes, adjust servings, check off items

### 5. Feature Showcase - Meal Categories
- Phone mockup showing the home screen with meal categories
- Title: "Organiza tus comidas por categoría"
- Show Breakfast, Lunch, Desserts categories

### 6. How It Works (3-step flow)
- Step 1: Describe your recipe → Step 2: AI generates it → Step 3: Shopping list ready
- Clean icons/illustrations for each step

### 7. About / Why Don Cheffy
- Founder story section (similar to Luffu's "From the founders" block)
- Mission: making cooking accessible and grocery shopping effortless

### 8. Final CTA + Waitlist Form
- Title: "Deja de improvisar en el súper"
- Email input + "Unirme" button to join waitlist
- Animated food avatars/emojis row (like Luffu's avatar strip)

### 9. Footer
- Logo, links, social media, copyright

## Design
- **Color palette**: Warm cream background (#FFF8F0), forest green buttons/accents (#4A7C59), dark green header (#2D4A3E) — matching the app's existing design
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif, large bold headlines
- **Animations**: Smooth scroll-triggered fade-ins, floating question bubbles in hero
- **Mobile-first responsive design**
- App screenshots embedded as phone mockups using the uploaded images
